Our Company

Frrand aims at reinventing the way people consider receiving a package by building a community of people who provide the service without cost. Frrand is a service that is offered by the same people who take advantage of it.


Our Team

Frrand is an early stage start-up currently in residence at the University of Toronto’s The Hub, an early stage innovation and business incubator. We’re a small but agile team of passionate engineers looking to solve a problem that we personally face daily: delivery as it is, sucks. The company was co-founded by Kenneth Ma, Terence Lee, Brandon Mowat, and Faisal Usmani.
